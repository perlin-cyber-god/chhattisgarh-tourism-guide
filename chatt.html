Discover the Soul of India

Welcome to Chhattisgarh, a land of mesmerizing natural beauty, ancient history, and vibrant tribal culture. Often called the "Rice Bowl of India," this state is covered in lush forests, dotted with majestic waterfalls, and home to unique wildlife. From the thundering Chitrakote Falls to the ancient temples of Sirpur, Chhattisgarh offers an unexplored paradise for every traveler.

Must-Visit Destinations

For Families

Chitrakote Waterfall

The whole family will be awestruck by the "Niagara of India." It's an easily accessible spot perfect for a day trip and amazing photos.

Nandan Van Zoo & Safari

Located near Raipur, this zoo and safari park is a hit with kids, offering a chance to see tigers, lions, and other wildlife up close.

Purkhouti Muktangan

An open-air museum that showcases the rich tribal culture of the state through life-like statues and dioramas. It's both fun and educational.

For College Students & Adventurers

Mainpat

Known as the 'Shimla of Chhattisgarh,' this hill station is perfect for trekking, exploring Tibetan refugee settlements, and enjoying cool weather.

Tirathgarh Falls

A stunning block-type waterfall cascading down in steps. The surrounding area in Kanger Valley National Park is great for light trekking.

Bastar Dussehra

Experience the world's longest festival (75 days)! It's a unique cultural immersion unlike any other, showcasing vibrant tribal traditions.

For Senior Citizens & Relaxation

Sirpur

A peaceful archaeological site with rich Buddhist and Hindu heritage. The flat terrain makes it easy to explore the stunning temple ruins.

Danteshwari Temple

One of the 52 Shakti Peethas, this ancient temple in Dantewada offers a serene and spiritual experience for pilgrims and visitors.

Rajim

Known as the 'Prayag of Chhattisgarh,' this holy town is famous for its beautiful temples at the confluence of three rivers. Ideal for a peaceful visit.

Scenic Viewpoints

Kailash Gufa Viewpoint

Located in Kanger Valley National Park, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the dense forest canopy. A serene spot for photographers.

Mainpat Sunrise Point

Witness a magical sunrise over the rolling hills of 'Chhattisgarh's Shimla'. An unforgettable experience for early risers.

Gadiya Mountain

A historic fort-mountain in Kanker that provides a stunning 360-degree view of the town and the surrounding landscape below.
